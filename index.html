Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between Light, Gold, and Full? +

Light: Focused on security. Includes GPS tracking, Admin Portal, and Remote Immobilization.
Gold: Focused on operations. Adds Keyless Lock/Unlock, offline Bluetooth, and the Manager App.
Full: The complete platform. Adds a White-label Customer App, CRM, Payment Processing, and full Telemetry.

How long does installation take? +

For the Light plan, installation is simpler and typically takes less than 1 hour. For Gold and Full (which involve more connections), professional installation is required, taking approximately 1 hour and 30 minutes per vehicle.

Why is hardwired better than the OBD dongles I see elsewhere? +
OBD dongles are visible and can be unplugged by a thief in seconds. IturanMob devices are installed covertly inside the dashboard, making them undetectable and impossible to disarm without tools.
Do customers need to download an app? +

For Full clients, yes‚Äîwe provide a white-label app (iOS/Android) or a web-based link. For Gold, you use the Admin App to generate access, while the end-user (driver) typically accesses the vehicle via a digital key link or bluetooth.

Is this compatible with Turo off-trip insurance? +
Yes! All our plans are fully compatible with Tint insurance requirements. Our system provides the precise GPS tracking data needed to validate off-trip status for claims.
Can we use our own branding? +
Yes. The Full plan is a fully white-label solution. The customer-facing mobile app and web booking portal will carry your logo and brand colors.
